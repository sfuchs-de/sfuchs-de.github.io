Welcome

I am a PhD student at Toulouse School of Economics and a Doctoral Fellow at the Institute for Advanced Study in Toulouse. This fall semester I will be visiting the International Economics Section at Dartmouth College. My work focuses on the interaction between international market integration and local labor markets and I expect to finish my PhD in 2018. My advisor is Thomas Chaney.
