Simon Fuchs, Research Economist

I am a research economist at the Atlanta Fed. You can find my CV here. I received my PhD from Toulouse School of Economics in 2018. I focus on the macroeconomic effects of the spatial distribution of economic activity. I work on topics in urban economics, transportation, and international economics. I am one of the co-organizers of the Atlanta International Economics Workshop.
